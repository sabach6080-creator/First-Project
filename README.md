# DevoraX


**Software Selling Website** — A modern, responsive web platform to browse, purchase, and manage software products, with dedicated pages for services, portfolio, contact, about, and career.  

---

##  What is DevoraX

DevoraX is a clean and user‑friendly website built to offer software products and related services. It’s designed both for customers to explore and buy software, and for admins/business‑owners to showcase offerings, show portfolio, provide company information, and manage contact and career opportunities.

##  Pages 

| Page | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Home** | Landing page that highlights the brand, main offerings, and gives a quick overview. |
| **Services** | Details of services offered — e.g. software sales, support, custom development, consultations. |
| **Portfolio** | Showcases past software products or projects — giving users a sense of quality and variety. |
| **About** | Information about the company/business — mission, vision, background, values, etc. |
| **Career** | Lists available jobs or collaboration opportunities. Potential employees or freelancers can learn how to apply. |
| **Contact** | Contact form or contact details allowing visitors to reach out for inquiries or support. |

##  Key Features

- Responsive design (works on desktop & mobile)  
- Clear navigation among all major pages (Home, Services, Portfolio, About, Career, Contact)  
- Portfolio section to showcase offerings or past works  
- Contact and career forms (or placeholders) for inquiries and job applications  
- Clean, professional layout — suitable for a startup or software selling business
